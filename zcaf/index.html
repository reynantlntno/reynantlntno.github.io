About Us

Welcome

At Zion Christian Academy Foundation, Inc., we are dedicated to providing a nurturing and academically challenging environment where students can grow in their faith, knowledge, and character. Located in the heart of our community, our school has become a beacon of educational excellence and spiritual development.

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate and inspire students to achieve their full potential in a Christ-centered environment. We strive to develop young minds and hearts, preparing them to lead lives of purpose and impact. Our commitment to academic excellence and spiritual growth ensures that each student receives a well-rounded education grounded in Christian values.

Our Vision

We envision a world where our students become influential leaders who embody the love and teachings of Jesus Christ. By fostering a culture of integrity, compassion, and intellectual curiosity, we aim to empower our students to make a positive difference in their communities and beyond.

Extracurricular Activities

We believe in the importance of a well-rounded education. Our students have the opportunity to participate in a variety of extracurricular activities, including sports, music, arts, and clubs. These activities help students develop leadership skills, teamwork, and personal interests beyond the classroom.

Join Our Community

We invite you to become a part of the Zion Christian Academy Foundation, Inc. family. Whether you are a prospective student, parent, or supporter, we welcome you to experience the warmth and excellence of our school community. Together, we can continue to build a bright future for our students and make a lasting impact in the world.
